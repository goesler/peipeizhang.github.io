Project 1:How many defending champions are there in the Olympic history?

In my opinion, there are two types of Olympic games, depending on whether referees score during the game. Games like track and field and swimming belong to one type. Diving and figure skating belong to the other category.
By analyzing the data, the project tries to find out how many defending champions there are in the Olympic history because it is an uneasy achievement. Also, I am courious to find outwhether there are obvious differences in the number of defending champions between the two types of games.

Data Source: IOC

I am not sure about the layout of the project. I want to find a way to present the data beautifully and efficiently.


Project 2:The return rate of online shopping

ABC reported that one-third of online shopping transactions get returned in the December, 2013. It is kind of interest, to me. Through this project, I want to compare the return rate of online shopping and physical store shopping. And I also want to compare the return rate of different categories such as clothes, food, toys, etc. to see if there are big differences. If so, I will also figure out the reasons behind it and maybe collect some possible solutions to it.

Data Source:I find it hard to find large scale of data on this issue. Maybe Department of Commerce has some data I need. Also, I am thinking about using the data of certain company Walmart, Macy's etc.

I think this project could be a blog. It contains several chats (or interactive chats), and also an article.
